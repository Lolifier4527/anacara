Our Story

A space to heal
and grow

I believe healing begins with being truly heard.

Ana Cara is a specialist wellness counselling practice dedicated to guiding individuals through life's most challenging transitions with compassion, clarity, and care. Whether you are navigating grief, anxiety, relationship difficulties, or a personal crossroads, this is a safe space for your journey.

With a trauma-informed, holistic approach, sessions are tailored to your unique needs — honouring both the pace and depth at which you are ready to grow.

Confidential & non-judgmental environment
Trauma-informed, person-centred approach
In-person & online sessions available
Qualified & accredited wellness counsellor
